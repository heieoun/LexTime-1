⚖️
UPL notice — LEXTIME does not provide legal advice. It is an administrative tool that automates activity logging, time capture, and billing-narrative drafting; every billed entry is reviewed and approved by a licensed attorney. (Designed for GDPR · CCPA · HIPAA · SOC 2 compliance.)
Activities captured by AI

Email, calendar, document, and call activity is collected in the background, and AI auto-matches each item to a matter/client. For every activity you choose Share or Private — private items are excluded from billing and auto-deleted locally. Nothing reaches the timesheet until you review and approve it.

Approved timesheet

AI-generated billing narratives. Edit them directly, or regenerate by tone (concise / detailed / formal). Open "✦ AI rationale" on any entry to see exactly which activities and models produced it.

Integrations

LEXTIME is not locked to any single accounting or management tool. Open API and two-way sync let it sit on top of your existing stack with no duplicate data entry. At pilot kickoff, just switch on the systems the firm already uses.

🔌
Lowering the adoption barrier — two-way (not one-way) sync means time approved in LEXTIME flows back into your existing PMS and accounting systems automatically. The Open API / webhooks are published, so custom integrations are possible. Contract-analysis is offered as an optional add-on module, not bundled into the base plan, to keep the core lean and ROI-focused.
